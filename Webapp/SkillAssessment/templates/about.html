About The Project


The project Skill Assessment is an extension of a broader vision of Dr. Philip L. Miller to revolutionize software developers evaluation and an authentic way to determine the competency of a software developer through authentic examination of their coding skills.

This project is conducted in his tenure at The LNM Institute of Technology in Jaipur, India. Dr. Miller is accompained by graduate student Yash Nag from The LNMIIT.
